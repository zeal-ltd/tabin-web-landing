Frequently Asked Questions

If you are hesitating, do not worry - we are here to explain everything you might want to know. Let us help!

Why is Tabin so great?

Tabin provides a modern, easy-to-use platform for self-service restaurant ordering. We provide a premium service with much lower rates than our competitors. We pride ourselves on being very flexible, modifying our solution to fit your business needs.

Can you integrate with exisiting PoS?

We are currently working on getting more and more PoS integrated with Tabin. If your PoS is a popular one, it is likely we have an integration available.

If you are a medium-large size restaurant chain, we may be able to a develop a custom integration with your exisiting system. Have a chat with us to figure out what we can do for you!

Do you provide table ordering or online ordering?

Our table ordering and online ordering is on our roadmap and we will look to make them available in the coming months.

Do you offer 24/7 support?

Yes, we currently offer 24/7 support on our premium and enterprise solutions.
